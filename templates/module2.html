Center Localization Error (pixels):
Measures the Euclidean distance between the center of the Ground Truth (GT) box and the center of the detected bounding box.
Lower values indicate higher localization accuracy.

Scale Deviation (%):
Measures the percentage difference between the area of the detected bounding box and the area of the Ground Truth box.
Lower values indicate better scale consistency with the true object size.
